About Us

Welcome to the Singapore Wildlife Foundation, where you are always welcome to interact, discover, and draw inspiration from the wonders of nature.

Here at the Singapore Wildlife Foundation, the beauties of nature are brought to life in vivid harmony. Take a fascinating trip with us as we joyfully announce the merging of the Jurong Bird Park and the Singapore Zoo under our prestigious foundation.

Take a deep dive into our world of exceptional conservation, where every action you take is in harmony with our aim to protect and honor the planet's vast biodiversity. Be ready to be enthralled by a tapestry of immersive experiences and breathtaking attractions that call out to the adventurer in you as you browse our website.

Attractions

Embark on an unforgettable adventure into the heart of nature's marvels at our combined attractions, where every moment sparks wonder and fascination. Delve into a realm teeming with an extraordinary array of species, each more captivating than the last, promising immersive experiences that will leave you spellbound.

Prepare to be enthralled as you step into our sanctuary of biodiversity, where the air hums with the whispers of the wild and every corner holds the promise of discovery. From the majestic roar of lions echoing across the plains to the delicate dance of vibrant avian beauties, our attractions beckon with tales of wonder waiting to be unveiled.

Join us on a journey beyond imagination, where the boundaries between observer and participant blur, inviting visitors of all ages to become explorers of the unknown. Traverse lush landscapes where every encounter is a brushstroke on the canvas of memory, etching stories of conservation and admiration for the natural world.

Come, unlock the secrets of the wild with us, and embark on an expedition of a lifetime. The wonders of wildlife await, ready to ignite your curiosity and ignite your spirit of adventure.
