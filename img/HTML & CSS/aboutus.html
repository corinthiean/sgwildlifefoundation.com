About Us

Welcome to the Singapore Wildlife Foundation, where you are always welcome to interact, discover, and draw inspiration from the wonders of nature.

Here at the Singapore Wildlife Foundation, the beauties of nature are brought to life in vivid harmony. Take a fascinating trip with us as we joyfully announce the merging of the Jurong Bird Park and the Singapore Zoo under our prestigious foundation.

Take a deep dive into our world of exceptional conservation, where every action you take is in harmony with our aim to protect and honor the planet's vast biodiversity. Be ready to be enthralled by a tapestry of immersive experiences and breathtaking attractions that call out to the adventurer in you as you browse our website.
